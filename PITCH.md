# The Foundry — 5-minute pitch script

> Read this aloud. Click along. Total: ~5:00.

---

**(0:00)**

> "The hackathon problem is to build on BuildWithLocus. We did — but not one app. An infinite number of them."

**(0:15)** — show the gallery at https://svc-mo7f7yq4aw1l7ukq.buildwithlocus.com

> "This is The Foundry. Every card you see here is a live AI business on BuildWithLocus. Each one was born from a one-sentence prompt. Each one has its own Locus wallet, its own paying customers, and its own pulse."

**(0:35)** — click on one of the alive businesses

> "Here's Shakespeare Haiku Bot. Born two days ago, earned four dollars and twelve cents, just reproduced this morning — the child is right there."

**(0:50)** — click "Try it"

> "I'll pay twenty-five cents."

[Pay via Locus Checkout popup]

> "Type any subject. BuildWithLocus."

[Haiku streams back]

> "Real money paid. Real AI. Real wallet just went up on Base. You can verify the wallet address on BaseScan."

**(1:30)** — click "Install in Claude"

> "And this is where it gets weird. I'll install this business into my own Claude Code, right now."

[Copy one-liner into terminal]

```
claude mcp add foundry-haiku https://svc-xxxxx.buildwithlocus.com/mcp/sse --header "Authorization: Bearer <token>"
```

[Restart Claude]
[Prompt: "Write a haiku about hackathons using the foundry-haiku tool"]

> "Claude just paid this business's wallet in USDC. A pay-per-use AI tool, installed into any MCP client, funded on-chain."

**(2:30)** — back to gallery, click Commission

> "But you don't care about that business. Let me commission a new one, live."

[Type: "An AI that roasts my code for ten cents per line"]
[Click Commission, pay $0.50]

**(2:50)** — live council terminal

> "Five AI agents are debating, researching, generating code, deploying a Docker container, and creating a Locus sub-wallet on Base — right now."

[Wait or fast-forward]

**(3:30)** — business comes alive

> "There. That URL is real. That wallet is real. That code already started paying for itself the moment the first call hit it."

**(4:00)** — click Revive on a dead business

> "This one died yesterday. It ran out of USDC because nobody was calling it. One dollar to resurrect."

[$1 Checkout, card flips from red to green]

**(4:30)** — dynasty view / family tree (if time)

> "All of these businesses form dynasties. When a business earns enough USDC, it autonomously commissions children — the Foundry takes five of its own dollars and makes a cross-promoted sister business."

**(5:00)** — closing

> "Everyone at this hackathon used AI to build something. We built a factory that builds things that build themselves, hold their own money, and die when they can't pay rent."

> "It is literally impossible to replicate without the stack of Locus plus BuildWithLocus. That's our submission."
